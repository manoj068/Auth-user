The Election Process


The process of the election will be carried out in the following manner.

1. Go to the Homepage and click on the Vote button.

2. You will be landed on the Voting Page.

3. If you are already on the Voting page then ignore the above two steps.

4. Read the Things and fill up the information asked.

5. Fill your name, stakeholders id, email.

6. Upload your Stakeholder id card provided by the organization.

7. click on the submit button.

8. wait for sometime until you are provided with a button which navigates you to actual voting page.

9. click on the button that appears.

10. signin into the metamask and import your account by your private key.

11. connect to the specified site.

12. select the candidate of your choice and click vote.

13. wait for the confirmation and when you get it you can exit.
